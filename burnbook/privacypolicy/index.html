What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application does not obtain any information when you download and use it. Registration is not required to use the Application.

Advertising

The Application displays interstitial advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

AdMob may collect and use certain information automatically to provide and improve advertising, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as the Advertising Identifier)
  • IP address
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • Ad interaction data
  • Diagnostic and performance data

This information is used to:

  • Display ads
  • Measure ad performance
  • Provide more relevant ads where permitted by the user

The Application does not have access to or control over the data collected by Google AdMob.

You can learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use its services here: policies.google.com/privacy

App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On devices running iOS 14.5 or later, the Application may request permission under Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework to allow Google AdMob to use the device’s advertising identifier for personalized ads.

Granting or denying this permission does not affect core app functionality.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device. Any approximate location used for advertising purposes is handled by Google AdMob based on IP address.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

The Application itself does not share any user-provided data with third parties.

However, third-party services used by the Application (specifically Google AdMob) may collect data automatically as described above.

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

What are my opt-out rights?

  • Deny App Tracking Transparency permission (on supported iOS versions)
  • Stop all data collection by uninstalling the Application using standard uninstall processes available on your device or app marketplace
